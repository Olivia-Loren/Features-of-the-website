...DISCOVER YOUR STYLE...


Clothes can help you feel like the best version of yourself. More than covering up, when discovering “what is my style?” an extension of self-expression is unlocked and reminds us that we can control how we feel about ourselves—when you look good, you feel good!

FEMININE!


Feminine style:

This doesn’t mean wearing pink or dressing in something “pretty.” A feminine look is about accentuating the female figure with form-fitting separates that flatter your body type.

Your wardroble must haves:

  • Well-tailored top
  • Fitted clothes
  • Delicate accent accessories

    • CLASSIC!

    Classic Style:

    What’s better than when comfort meets style? Nothing. This look combines the best of both worlds with a fashionably laidback sensibility for those that don’t want to look like they’re trying too hard.

    Your wardroble must haves:

    • Crisp white tee
    • Chic (and opaque) leggings
    • Simple, all-white lace ups

      • MODERN!!

      Modern Style::

      This look is all about keeping your wardrobe no-fuss and fresh—less is more. Think Parisian cool-girl-chic with elevated basics, in hushed tones, for an ensemble that is timeless not over the top.

      Your wardroble must haves:

      • Relaxed trousers
      • On-the-go tote
      • Luxe-looking mules

        • BOHO!

        Boho Style:

        The key to this free-flowing, effortless style? Layering. Airy silhouettes are topped off with structured jackets and stacked accessories for a romantic look that’s carefree and casual.

        Your wardroble must haves:

        • Long, sweeping maxi dress
        • Over-the-shoulder carryall
        • Wear-with-anything ankle boots
